# Strategic Super IC: Vision & Goal

## Objective

Become a **Strategic Super IC** (Individual Contributor)—an engineer who operates at the intersection of deep technical execution and product/org strategy, driving outsized impact without a formal management title.

## What It Means

A Strategic Super IC:

- **Owns the "why"** — Understands business goals, user needs, and org priorities; aligns technical decisions with them.
- **Thinks in systems** — Designs for scale, maintainability, and long-term value, not just immediate delivery.
- **Influences without authority** — Shapes direction through clarity, data, and persuasion; earns trust across teams.
- **Ships high-leverage work** — Focuses on initiatives that unlock others or create durable value.
- **Mentors and elevates** — Shares knowledge, raises the bar, and helps others succeed.

## Path Forward

1. **Deepen domain expertise** — Master the product, users, and technical stack; become the go-to person for hard questions.
2. **Communicate strategy** — Write docs, run reviews, and present in a way that aligns stakeholders and creates shared understanding.
3. **Pick high-impact projects** — Prioritize work that compounds: platforms, abstractions, and initiatives that enable future work.
4. **Build relationships** — Collaborate across eng, product, and design; understand their constraints and goals.
5. **Document and share** — Capture decisions, tradeoffs, and learnings so the org can scale without you in the room.

## Success Metrics

- Recognized as a trusted technical and strategic voice.
- Work that others build on; systems that last.
- Clear articulation of "why" behind major technical and product choices.
- Influence that extends beyond immediate team and codebase.
